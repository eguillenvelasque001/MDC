About this movie

The film follows the intergalactic despot Thanos as he attempts to collect all six Infinity Stones—artifacts of unimaginable power that control different aspects of existence: Space, Reality, Power, Soul, Mind, and Time.

  • Thanos's Goal: He believes the universe is overpopulated and its resources are infinite. His "solution" is to use the stones to wipe out half of all life in the universe with a single snap of his fingers to restore "balance."

  • The Heroes' Stand: For the first time, the Avengers (Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, etc.) must join forces with the Guardians of the Galaxy, Doctor Strange, and the forces of Wakanda to stop him.

  • The Stakes: The movie is famous for its high stakes and "dark" ending, where the heroes face their most devastating defeat yet.
